# TEF Mock Test

The **TEF Canada** exam is one of the official French language tests accepted for Canadian immigration. Success on the TEF requires more than strong French skills—it requires familiarity with the exam structure, effective time management, and continuous practice under realistic conditions.

Mocko's TEF Mock Test reproduces the official exam experience across Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking. After every attempt, you receive detailed feedback and performance analysis to help you focus your preparation where it matters most.
