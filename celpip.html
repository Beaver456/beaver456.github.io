What Is CELPIP?

CELPIP is an English language proficiency test used by candidates who need to demonstrate practical English ability for Canadian immigration, citizenship or professional purposes.

The exam evaluates Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking through real-life English communication tasks.

How Technology Helps CELPIP Preparation

Technology allows candidates to practice under realistic conditions, track performance and understand their weaknesses more clearly.

AI-powered analysis can identify repeated mistakes, timing issues and skill gaps that are difficult to notice through traditional study alone.
