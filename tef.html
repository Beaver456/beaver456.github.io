What Is TEF?

TEF, or Test d’Évaluation de Français, is a French language proficiency exam used to assess a candidate's ability to understand and use French in practical situations.

For many candidates, TEF preparation requires grammar, vocabulary, comprehension, speaking ability and strong familiarity with the exam format.

How Technology Helps TEF Preparation

Digital practice makes TEF preparation more structured. Students can simulate exam conditions, review results and understand which skills need improvement.

AI analysis helps learners move beyond random study by showing patterns in errors and highlighting areas that need focused attention.

How Mocko Helps Students Improve

Mocko helps TEF candidates practice with realistic mock tests and receive clear performance insights after each attempt.

This allows learners to build confidence, improve accuracy and prepare for the real exam with a more focused strategy.
