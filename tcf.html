What Is TCF?

TCF, or Test de Connaissance du Français, is a French language proficiency test that measures how well a candidate understands and uses French.

It is commonly used for immigration, academic admission and professional purposes depending on the version required.

How Technology Helps TCF Preparation

Technology helps TCF candidates practice more consistently and measure progress over time.

AI-powered tools can analyze performance, identify weak skills and guide students toward more effective preparation.

How Mocko Helps Students Improve

Mocko offers realistic TCF mock tests that help learners become familiar with the exam structure and improve through detailed analysis.

Students can use feedback to understand mistakes, track improvement and prepare with greater confidence.
